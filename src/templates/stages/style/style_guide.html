The Fractional Grid

Fractions

The Fractional Grid is

surprise, surprise
built upon the concept of fractions. In it you'll layer .row's containing fractional .col's like:
  • .col.full
  • .col.sixth.fifth
  • .col.fifth.four
  • .col.fourth.three
  • .col.third.two
  • .col.fifth.three
  • .col.half
  • .col.fifth.two
  • .col.third
  • .col.fourth
  • .col.fifth
  • .col.sixth

Responsiveness

The other main benefit of the fractional grid is how it responds to an infinite variety of screen widths. Flannel CSS is sensitive to screen widths, allowing you the content/markup author to dial in the layout for just about any screen without writing a lick of CSS.

    XSM
    <240px - Remember flip phones?
    SML
    <360px - Most modern smartphones
    MED
    <536px - Phablets, Plusses, Notes, VGA
    LRG
    <768px - Tablets, iPads, SVGA
    XLG
    <1024px - Laptops, XGA
    XXL
    <1280px - Big laptops
    UHD
    <1920px - Big desktop displays, HDTVs
