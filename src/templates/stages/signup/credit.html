What's a soft credit check?

A soft credit check is one that does not affect your credit score. A soft credit check is recorded in your credit history, but is only visible to you and not visible to lenders.

We do this to determine what financing we can offer you. We’ll walk through your financing options with you once your professional design is complete so we know your exact system size/production.

If you’re not interested in financing and prefer to purchase your system, skip this step and give us a call at (877) 670-7652 to speak with a solar consultant.
